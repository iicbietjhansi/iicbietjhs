About Us

Mission

IIC will focus on creating complete ecosystem which will foster the culture of Innovation across all educational institutions from ideas generation to pre-incubation, incubation and graduating from the incubator as successful start-ups. IIC will actively engage various central and state agencies to ensure that dedicated budget is allocated for innovation related activities. Ministry of HRD has established ‘MRHD’s Innovation Cell’ with the mandate to work closely with our Incubation Center to encourage the creative energy of our student population to work on new ideas and innovation and promote them to create start-ups and entrepreneurial ventures.
