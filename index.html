About Us

Mission

We focus on creating an ecosystem which will foster the culture of Innovation across educational institutions from ideas generation to pre-incubation, incubation and graduating from the incubator as successful start-ups. We actively engage with various central and state agencies to ensure that dedicated budget is allocated for innovation related activities. Ministry of HRD has established ‘MRHD’s Innovation Cell’ with the mandate to work closely with our Incubation Center to encourage the creative energy of our students to work on new ideas and innovation and promote them to come up start-ups and entrepreneurial ventures.

Our Mentors

Prof. Jitendra Kumar Sharma

Director

Prof. Jitendra Kumar Sharma is currently working as the Director of Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering and Technology (BIET), Jhansi. He is a very dynamic leader and also previously served as Director of Government Engineering College like REC Mainpuri, Bikaner, Ajmer, and Manpur. . He has been a renowned researcher and administrator who led the Civil, Petroleum, Petrochemical, and Chemical Engineering departments at Rajasthan Technical University, Kota as head of the department. With over 35 years of teaching and research experience, he has guided more than eight Ph.D. scholars and supervised many M. Tech. students. An alumnus of HBTI Kanpur, REC Kurukshetra, and IIT Kanpur, Prof. Jitendra Kumar Sharma has published 100+ research papers in reputable journals and conferences, holds several patents, and has received multiple research awards. He is a Fellow of the Institution of Engineers (India), Life Member of the Indian Geotechnical Society, and a member of ISTE.

Prof. Dr. Atul Kumar Dwivedi

Vice Pressident

Prof. Dr. Atul Kumar Dwivedi is a distinguished academician and accomplished researcher with over 12 years of experience in teaching, mentoring, and institutional leadership. He is currently serving at BIET Jhansi, where he holds multiple key roles including Faculty, Deputy Training & Placement Officer, Faculty Incharge of the IoT Experience Centre, and Vice President of the Innovation & Incubation Centre. The Best Teacher Awardee, he is widely recognized for his expertise in Electrical and Electronic Systems, Signal Processing, Artificial Intelligence Solutions, he also holds a Doctorate in Signal Processing and a post-graduate degree in VLSI Design. His academic and research work focuses on integrating AI and IoT with Digital Signal Processing, Biomedical Applications, and Integrated Circuits. Prof. Dr. Atul Kumar Dwivedi has made valuable contributions to government-sponsored projects such as Anuvad—a national initiative for translating technical textbooks into Hindi—and a project focusing on Depression Analysis using AI and signal processing. He has published numerous papers in prestigious international journals and regularly delivers workshops and technical training sessions.

Dr. Sunil Kumar Rajput

Deputy Coordinator

Dr. Sunil Kumar Rajput is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering & Technology (BIET), Jhansi. He holds a Ph.D. in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee. Dr. Rajput's academic and research work primarily focuses on hot deformation behavior, mechanical behavior of materials, and materials processing techniques. With over 65 research publications in reputed national and international journals, he has made significant contributions to the field of mechanical and materials engineering. He has successfully guided 28 M.Tech dissertations and 2 Ph.D. scholars, and is currently supervising 1 ongoing Ph.D. candidate. Beyond his teaching and research roles, he had served as the Assistant Dean Academics at BIET Jhansi, where he plays a crucial role in academic planning and administration.
