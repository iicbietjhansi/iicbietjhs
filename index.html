About Us

Mission

We focus on creating an ecosystem which will foster the culture of Innovation across educational institutions from ideas generation to pre-incubation, incubation and graduating from the incubator as successful start-ups. We actively engage with various central and state agencies to ensure that dedicated budget is allocated for innovation related activities. Ministry of HRD has established ‘MRHD’s Innovation Cell’ with the mandate to work closely with our Incubation Center to encourage the creative energy of our students to work on new ideas and innovation and promote them to come up start-ups and entrepreneurial ventures.

Our Mentors

Prof. Deependra Singh

Director

Joined Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering and Technology (BIET) Jhansi on 7 November 2023. He is a well-known Electrical Engineer and Professor in India. He was also selected as Lecturer in Higher College of Technology, Muscat, Oman (Saudi Arabia) for an year. Prof. Singh earned his PHD from U P Technical University, Lucknow, M.E. from IIT Roorkee and B.Tech from HBTI (now HBTU) Kanpur. He has supervised 10 M.Tech and 15 B.Tech projects. Apart from this, he has published 12 International and 4 National Journals. He has a strong quality research in publications of reputed journals. Prof. Singh has rich experience in the fields of academics, research and now leading BIET with his strong administrative qualities.

Prof. (Dr.) Shahanaz Ayub

President

Prof. (Dr.) Shahanaz Ayub is Professor in Electronics and Communication Engineering Department. She is also the Dean of Research and Development. Dr. Ayub is President of Institute Innovation Council and Coordinator of Innovation and Incubation Cell. She has keen interest in Outcome Based Education and is actively involved in NBA Accreditation of UG programs. She is also a member of the women's grievance redressal cell. She has been selected for the prestigious National Award to Teachers – Best Teacher Award, 2024, which will be presented by the Hon'ble President of India on September 5, 2024, at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi. She was previously honored with the AICTE VISVESVARAYA BEST TEACHER AWARD 2021 by Hon'ble Education Minister Shri Dharmendra Pradhan ji.

Bundelkhand Innovation and Incubation Centre of Foundation, is an MHRD approved council established in BIET, Jhansi to promote start up culture in the Bundelkhand region.

Owing to its location in the Bundelkhand region, applicants get an additional 50% seeding grant for the development of their idea.
