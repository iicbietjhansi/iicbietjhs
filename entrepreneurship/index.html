Lets first see how Startup is Incubated

We will be looking upon the basic steps involved in startup Incubations

  • Step - 01 Ideation

    Ideation is the first stage of the startup incubation process, during which the founders develop and validate their business idea. This stage includes market research, competitor analysis, and customer development. The goal of this stage is to ensure that the startup has a viable business model and a clear value proposition.

  • Step - 02 Development

    Development is the second stage of the startup incubation process, during which the startup team builds the product or service. This stage includes product development, marketing, and sales. The goal of this stage is to launch a minimum viable product (MVP) that can be used by early adopters.

  • Step - 03 Launch

    Launch is the third stage of the startup incubation process, during which the startup team launches the product or service to the market. This stage includes product launch, marketing, and sales. The goal of this stage is to achieve market traction and grow the business.

Have a question? Find it here

  • What support does the Innovation and Incubation Cell offer?

    Our cell provides comprehensive support, including funding opportunities, expert mentorship, collaborative workspaces, and access to interns, fostering a dynamic environment for innovation.

  • How can I apply for incubation for my startup?

    To apply, visit our website and contact us.We carefully review each application to identify promising ventures.

  • Are there any eligibility criteria for startups seeking incubation?

    While criteria may vary, we generally look for innovative ideas, a viable business plan, and a dedicated team.Contact Us for more details.

  • Is there financial support available for startups?

    Yes, we offer financial support in the form of grants and funding. Our goal is to help startups overcome financial barriers and thrive.

  • Can I apply for incubation if my startup is still in the idea stage?

    Yes, we encourage early-stage startups with innovative ideas to apply. Contact us for guidance on submitting your application.
